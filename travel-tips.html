Four seasons in South Korea

What is the best time to visit South Korea?

Spring

Spring is one of the best times to visit South Korea due to its mild weather and beautiful scenery. Cherry blossoms bloom across the country, and festivals such as the Jinhae Cherry Blossom Festival attract many visitors. The temperatures are comfortable for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

See itinerary

Summer

Summer in South Korea is hot and humid, with occasional heavy rainfall during the monsoon season. Despite this, it is a popular time for beach destinations like Busan and island trips to Jeju. Summer also features lively festivals and vibrant city life.

See itinerary

Autumn

Autumn is considered the best season to travel because of its cool temperatures and clear skies. The countryside is filled with stunning autumn foliage, making it ideal for hiking and photography. It is also the season of Chuseok, offering cultural experiences and traditional celebrations.

See itinerary

Winter

Winter is cold, especially in northern regions, with snowfall in many areas. This season is perfect for winter sports such as skiing and snowboarding, particularly in Gangwon Province. Winter festivals and scenic snow-covered landscapes provide a unique travel experience.

Transportation Methods

🚌

Buses

Buses operate within cities and for long-distance travel between towns. Express and intercity buses are comfortable, punctual, and often cheaper than trains, making them a reliable way to explore the country.

🚅

Subway

🚉

Trains (KTX & Regular)

The KTX high-speed trains connect major cities quickly, cutting travel time significantly. Regular trains also reach smaller towns, providing a convenient and scenic way to travel across South Korea.
